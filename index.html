Welcome to "Songs of Diridum"!

This is technical demo demonstrating the Web Audio API. The game world is based on the upcoming game "Legend of Diridum" built on the Goo Engine.

How to use

  • WASD and arrow keys to move around.
  • Mouse to look around.
  • The big "Play" button (in the bottom-right corner) toggles sound on/off.
  • Open the sound controls (next to the Play button) to toggle individual channels on/off, toggle low-pass filter, or adjust individual channel volume.
